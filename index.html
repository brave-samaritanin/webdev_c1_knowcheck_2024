About Me

I am Captain Jack Sparrow, the legendary pirate whose compass points to the nearest rum bottle instead of true north. The only man to have ever walked the plank and turned it into a catwalk. I am so confident, that even my parrot wears an eyepatch just to keep up with my style. When I enter a tavern, the bar stools slide themselves over to make room for my inevitable entrance, and the sea itself parts ways to let me pass, probably to avoid getting tangled in my luxurious dreadlocks. Captain Jack Sparrow, the pirate so cool, that even the Kraken gives me a respectful nod as he sails by.
